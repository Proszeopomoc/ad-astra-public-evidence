# Scientific Limitations

This public package is not a planet-discovery claim.

The benchmark is catalog-level and public-redacted. It does not replace:
- centroid analysis,
- pixel-level vetting,
- ephemeris matching,
- odd/even transit analysis,
- secondary eclipse checks,
- Bayesian inference,
- MCMC validation,
- expert astrophysical review.

The visualizations are data-derived projections intended to demonstrate separability and stream structure. They are not physical maps of space and do not confirm object classes.
